Leadership

Captain & Lead Engineer
VEX Robotics Team 21052A
Nov 2022 - Apr 2025

I joined my school's VEX robotics team in its infancy, in 2022. Since then, I served as captain of Five Rings Robotics (Team 21052A) for the 2023/24 season, leading the team to qualify for the World Finals in Dallas of that season. The year after, I served as Lead Engineer for the A team, introducing a new drivetrain setup and improved techniques for stability and reliabilty that helped us develop significantly over the previous year.

  • Directed a six-member robotics team to qualify for and compete in the 2024 VEX World Finals in Dallas, Texas.
  • Directed engineering across mechanical, electrical, and coding decisions during the project lifecycle.
  • Co-authored and maintained a 100+ page engineering notebook documenting design rationale and product testing./li>
  • Expanded team presence through social media management and STEM outreach programs in the local community.

This was one of the most formative experiences of my time in high school. It introduced me to engineering and robotics, but more importantly, connected me with dozens of amazing, driven, talented students who I got to know across that three year period. I also got to know and get closer to our coach and mentor, Mr. Turland, who made it all possible and was always there for us be it as a teacher, mentor, or even just as an emotional support and mediator.
